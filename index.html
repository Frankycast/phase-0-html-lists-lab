• 2 slices of bread
  • 4 slices of cheese
    • chedder
    • mozarella
    • pepper jack
  • 1 tbsp of butter
  1. Spread butter on bread & frying pan
  2. Place bread in frying pan & fry
  3. Add cheese on top of bread
  4. Cover with second side of bread
  5. Turn over & fry for 2 minutes
